About

SPiCE provides easy access to visualization and classification methods for sets of protein sequences. It can be used to calculate sequence-based protein features, visualize feature data, and construct protein classifiers.

SPiCE has been developed as part of my PhD in the Delft Bioinformatics Lab at Delft University of Technology. The software was initialy used for the construction of a classifier that predicts successful high-level secretion of proteins in Asperigillus niger (1). To make feature exploration and classifier construction methods generally accessible, we developed the SPiCE website as a user-friendly graphical user interface to that software. SPiCE is described in the following paper (2).

The website can be explored without registration. In that case your data is not password protected and data will not be stored for more than a month. A registered account can be created providing you with a protected workspace. You need to register with a valid email address and a password. A verification link will be send to you in order to activate your account.

The website performance best in Google Chrome. Both Firefox and Opera were tested, however, less extensive. Please avoid using Internet Explorer (espessially the older versions), not all functionalities work in this browser. Please contact me in case of any other issues.
