About

SPiCE provides easy access to visualization and classification methods for a labeled set of protein sequences. You can use it to calculate global protein features, visualize feature data, and construct protein classifiers.

The software was initialy used for the construction of a classifier that predicts successful high-level secretion of proteins in Asperigillus niger (1). Afterwards, SPiCE was developed to make this general classifier construction approach available to others.

Exploring sequence characteristics related to high-level production of secreted proteins in Aspergillus niger. B.A. van den Berg, M.J.T. Reinders, M. Hulsman, L. Wu, H.J. Pel, J.A. Roubos, D. de Ridder (2012) PLoS ONE, Volume 7, Issue 10, e45869.
